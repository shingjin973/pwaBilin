About

Our Philosophy

At Bilin Academy, we support the ​approach of content and language integrated learning (CLIL​)​, commit to ​creating enjoyable learning experiences​, and believe in​ ​educational innovation​.

We work to connect passionate​, ​dedicated teachers with little learners across continents. We develop unique and creative online classes to cultivate our next generation into multilingual global citizens

Our Founding

Bilin Academy (formerly Bilin Studio) started as an experiment, offering online drawing classes in English to children in China. We believe by incorporating language learning into drawing lessons, children can acquire the target language naturally in a fun and engaging environment. In the past two years, we have created more than 30 different series of drawing classes with varied topics and different levels, taught in both English and Mandarin. We are continuing to expand our class offerings by collaborating with other extracurricular schools to provide calligraphy, chess, go classes, and much more. Now we have students from the US, China, Singapore, the UK, Japan, and other countries, all connecting and learning together in the same virtual classroom.

Our Classes

We strive for creative, effective,​ ​and engaging class experiences. We choose topics that are interesting to kids yet unique. We keep our classes small to ensure there is plenty of interaction between students and teachers.

We value our teachers as our greatest assets​ ​in providing professional knowledge and creating great learning experiences for our students.

We partner with other organizations to provide quality online classes as well. Our partners bring their expertise and an added variety of subjects.
